We are IT-parts, and we give technology a second chance. We focus on sustainable and smart IT solutions that help extend the lifespan of laptops, computers, and their components. We believe that quality technology deserves to function for as long as possible. That’s why we collaborate with partners specialized in refurbishing and reusing devices, contributing to the reduction of electronic waste and the protection of the environment.

We offer our services to customers across Europe. Thanks to reliable logistics and a personalized approach, we ensure the availability of IT solutions for both individuals and businesses. Our goal is to combine quality, accessibility, and a responsible approach to technology—simply, smartly, and sustainably. 🌱

Our Facilities in Prague

We are based in Prague, Czech Republic, where we operate a warehouse with an area of 650 square meters. In this space, we have a wide range of computers and components in stock, ensuring fast availability.

Our facilities also include a warehouse with computers from our partner company pocitace24.cz, which specializes in purchasing, refurbishing, and renting IT equipment. Thanks to this, we are able to offer comprehensive IT solutions and cover a wide range of our customers’ needs.

Who We Are and Why We Were Founded

IT-parts.cz was created based on the experience of the company pocitace24.cz, where in our daily work we encountered equipment that had been discarded as unsuitable, damaged, or incomplete. However, these were often devices and components that still had potential and could be repaired or reused.

We therefore decided to take this further and focus on making original spare parts and components accessible to anyone who wants to repair technology—whether professionally or even at home. Our goal is to offer high-quality parts at the best possible prices and enable people to extend the lifespan of their devices without the need for costly replacements.

We believe that repair should be accessible to everyone. With our approach, we not only help reduce costs but also support sustainability and the reduction of electronic waste 🌱

How We Got Here

2012 July 10

Foundation of pocitace24.cz

We started with refurbished IT equipment. We gained know-how, service experience, and a deep understanding of laptops and computers in real-world operation.

2022 December 26

Launch of IT-parts.eu

A standalone project focused on selling spare parts was created. At the same time, we actively sell on online marketplaces (Aukro, Allegro, eBay), where we are building a strong reputation and earning hundreds of positive reviews.

2025 January 1

Launch of IT-parts.cz

After experiencing security incidents and the compromise of the original e-shop, we decided to make a fresh start.

IT-parts.cz is a modern, fully secured e-shop, built with a focus on stability, security, and long-term customer trust.
